## Problem Statement

Sierra Leone's agricultural sector faces significant challenges:

- **Market Access Gap:** Over 60% of smallholder farmers lack direct market access, forcing them to sell to middlemen at below-market prices
- **Post-Harvest Losses:** Farmers lose 30-40% of produce due to delayed market connections
- **Price Information Asymmetry:** No access to real-time market prices, making farmers vulnerable to exploitation
- **Geographic Isolation:** Rural farmers in districts like Kailahun, Kono, and Pujehun struggle to reach urban markets

## Solution Overview

**SaloneBazar** is a complete web solution that addresses these challenges by providing:

- Direct marketplace connecting farmers with buyers
- Real-time market price information in Sierra Leonean Leones (SLL)
- Farmer dashboard for inventory and order management
- Mobile-responsive design accessible via basic smartphones
- Free registration with zero commission fees
