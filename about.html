About SaloneBazar

Empowering Sierra Leone's agricultural community through fair trade and technology

Our Mission

To transform Sierra Leone's agricultural sector by providing farmers with direct market access, fair prices, and digital tools that increase their income and reduce post-harvest losses.

Our Vision

A Sierra Leone where every farmer has equal access to markets, technology, and opportunities to thrive, creating food security and economic prosperity for all.

Our Story

SaloneBazar was founded in 2024 by a group of Sierra Leonean agri-tech enthusiasts who witnessed firsthand the struggles of local farmers. Despite producing high-quality crops, farmers were forced to sell to middlemen at unfairly low prices while buyers in Freetown paid premium rates for the same produce.

Recognizing this market inefficiency, we built SaloneBazar - a digital marketplace that directly connects farmers with buyers across all 16 districts of Sierra Leone. Our platform eliminates unnecessary intermediaries, provides real-time market prices, and empowers farmers with data-driven insights.

Today, SaloneBazar serves over 500+ registered farmers, facilitates thousands of transactions monthly, and is recognized as a Digital Public Good (DPG) for its contribution to sustainable agriculture in West Africa.
