❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a farmer on SaloneBazar? ▼
Simply click on the "Register" tab in our navigation menu, fill out the registration form with your details, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation within 24 hours.
Is there any fee to sell on SaloneBazar? ▼
No! Registration and listing are completely FREE for Sierra Leone farmers. We're committed to supporting local agriculture.
How do I get paid for my products? ▼
Payments can be made via mobile money (Orange Money, Afrimoney), bank transfer, or cash on delivery. We'll guide you through the process.
What areas do you serve? ▼
We currently serve all major districts including Western Area, Bo, Kenema, Makeni, Kono, Kailahun, and Port Loko. We're expanding to more areas soon!
How can I report a problem with my order? ▼
Use our contact form above or call our support hotline at +232 77 000 234. Our team will assist you within 24 hours.
